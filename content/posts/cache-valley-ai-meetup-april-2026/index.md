### Event Details: Cache Valley AI Community
- **Date**: Friday, April 24th, 2026
- **Time**: 12:00 PM (MST)
- **Location**: Beehive Grill, Logan, UT

## Event Overview
Come learn and connect on AI, machine learning, and triangulate the future! The Cache Valley AI Meetup is getting together over lunch for an open conversation about what's happening in AI and ML, what we're all working on, and where things are headed.

Whether you're building with AI every day, just starting to explore the space, or simply curious about what all the fuss is about, you're welcome at the table.

## Why Attend This Meetup?
Show up if you're:
- **Developers and engineers** working with (or curious about) AI and machine learning
- **Students and educators** interested in the practical side of ML
- **Tech enthusiasts** who want to connect with others in Cache Valley's AI community
- **Anyone** interested in where AI is going and how to be part of it
